# Form Collections

## serviceRequests

Used by `/request`.

Fields include customer contact info, service address, selected service, preferred date/time, promo code, pets, access notes, request details, laundry preferences, consent, and text/email consent.

## helperApplications

Used by `/helpers` part-time helper form.

Collects contact info, availability, services, experience, transportation, references, and consent to future screening steps.

Does not collect SSNs, ID photos, bank info, or full background-check information.

## partnerApplications

Used by `/helpers` independent contractor / partner provider form.

Collects business name, contact, service type, website, service area, license/insurance notes, capacity, and consent to review.

## contactMessages

Used by `/contact`.
