# NestHelper Launch Checklist

## Business readiness

- LLC formed and active
- Registered agent active
- EIN received
- WA business license / UBI active
- Business bank account active
- Insurance active before sending helpers into homes
- Sales tax workflow confirmed: Stripe automatic tax stays off; manual sales tax is added only by admin when needed
- Business email and phone active

## Website readiness

- Domain connected
- Logo and badge displaying correctly
- Contact info updated
- Service area updated
- Request form tested
- Helper application tested
- Partner application tested
- Policy pages reviewed
- Mobile header/menu tested
- Footer links tested
- Stripe test payments tested
- Firestore submissions tested

## Operations

- Internal request review checklist
- Customer approval message template
- Checkout/payment message template
- Laundry final dry weight checklist
- Completion/follow-up message template
- Damage/incident reporting process
- Helper onboarding flow
- Partner vetting flow

## Public claims

Publish “NestHelper LLC” and “insured” language only when those are actually active.
