# NestHelper Cost Planning

Verify all costs before paying. This worksheet is for planning, not legal/tax/accounting advice.

## Startup/admin

- Domain: often around $10-$25/year depending registrar and TLD.
- Washington LLC filing: budget around $180+ filing/processing costs.
- Washington annual report: budget around $70/year.
- WA business license application: budget around $50+ local endorsements if applicable.
- Registered agent: often $50-$150/year.
- Business email: often $7-$15/user/month.

## Technology

- Next.js hosting: Vercel free to start, paid plan when needed.
- Firebase/Firestore: free tier to start, pay-as-you-go when usage grows.
- Stripe: standard online card processing is commonly around 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction in the U.S.; Stripe Tax may add a separate percentage fee for taxable transactions.
- Domain email/text tools: budget depends on provider.

## Operations

- Background checks: budget roughly $30-$125+ per helper depending package/add-ons.
- Insurance: budget varies widely. Get quotes for general liability, bonding/employee dishonesty, bailee/customer property for laundry, hired/non-owned auto for errands, and workers comp if employees.
- Payroll software: often base monthly fee + per-employee fee.
- Helper pay: price services to support legal wages, payroll burden, sick leave, workers comp, travel/admin time, supplies, rework reserve, and profit.

## Pricing rule of thumb

Do not price NestHelper like a casual Facebook helper. Price it like a managed, vetted, insured, parent-focused service with real accountability.
